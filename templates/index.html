Welcome to Mario&Luigi's Pizzeria

Our story begins in the late 90's, in Napoli. This why we introduced the Pizza Napoli, to remind us about our journey. We met at a pizza contest, Mario was one of the judges and Luigi a contestant. When Mario tasted Luigi's pizza, a business idea immediately stroke him. Besides that, a genuine connection was built between the two, which helped them grow this business.

Why Amsterdam you would ask? Because both of them were in love with the city and wanted to share an authentic italian experience with other cultures.
